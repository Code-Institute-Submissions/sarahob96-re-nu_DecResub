Our Story

"RENU Apparel was created with sustainability in mind"

Sarah and Alice created RENU APPAREL back in 2020 when the world was suffering from both COVID
and global warming impacts. There was a gap in the irish market for sustainable wear that was comfortable
and afforable. Renu was inspired by many people and their work from home lifestyle. Fitness has also become more important for people.
Since RENU was brought to life in the irish market, it has become popular worldwide, now shipping internationally.

RENU Apparel was created with sustainability in mind.
Our products are investment pieces that really will be apart of your "WORKOUT UNIFORM" for years to come.
